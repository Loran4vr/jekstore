## Video Specs

- **Resolution:** 720x1280 (vertical)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 + AAC)
- **Style:** Stock footage + white text on black box + voiceover + music
- **Transitions:** Black fade between scenes

---

## How to Create New Video

1. Download stock footage from Pexels
2. Create 4-5 scenes with different cuts
3. Add white text with black box (fontsize 50-80)
4. Generate voiceovers with Google TTS
5. Add background music (sine wave)
6. Combine with fade transitions
